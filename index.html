Learn How to Play Tower of Mobs

~~~~What is Tower of Mobs?~~~~

  • The goal is to move all the mobs from the left tower to the right tower, adhering to the following rules
  • Move only one image at a time
  • An image can not be placed on top of the image prior
  • All images MUST connect on the last tower
