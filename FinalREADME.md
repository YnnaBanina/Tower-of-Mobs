### What is Tower of Mobs? 
---
Tower of Mobs is my take on the Tower of Hanoi puzzle game. It contains 6 disks that the player has to move to the last tower. 

*Can you move the mobs to the last tower?*

### Technology Used 
---
- JavaScript 
- Procreate
